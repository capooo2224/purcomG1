Impact Report

After Activity Report (IV)

Project Title: Cyber Watch: Empowering the Residents of Sitio Sampalukan in Media Literacy & Cybersecurity

Objectives

  • To develop the spirit of volunteerism in caring for others.
  • To pursue active involvement of students, faculty members, administrators, and non-teaching personnel in their area of expertise.
  • To conduct sustainable programs and services responsive to the needs, conditions, challenges, and opportunities in the area of H.E.L.P.E.R.S.

Summary of Activities

The team successfully conducted a seminar on basic cybersecurity, focusing on practical strategies to stay safe online. Participants were introduced to essential concepts such as protecting personal information, recognizing online threats, and practicing safe browsing habits. This activity aimed to raise awareness and strengthen digital safety among attendees.

In addition, a seminar on basic media and information literacy was carried out. This session emphasized the importance of critically evaluating information sources, identifying misinformation, and developing responsible media consumption habits. By equipping participants with these skills, the seminar promoted informed decision-making and responsible engagement in the digital space.

Awareness and Education

The activity helped strengthen awareness of cybersecurity and media literacy among the participants.

Engagement and Participation

We tried to be more relatable and casual with the participants and did our best to encourage them to raise questions about the topic, and we believe our message was clear.

Evaluation Data (XI)

Question Responses / Summary
1. Nasa inyong kagustuhan ba ang prenstasyon ng aming pangkat? Likert scale 1 - highest, 5 - lowest. Among the four participants, two answered 1 and the rest answered 2 and 3. Suggesting that the seminar was satisfactory.
2. Maayos ba na i-pahayag ang mga gustong pahayag ng bawat mananalita sa pangkat? Likert scale 1 - highest, 5 - lowest. Among the four participants, three answered 1 and the last one answered 3. Suggesting that each speaker's message was understood and satisfactory.
3. Ma aliwalas ba ang pag-tayo ng pangkat sa mga kagamitan ng seminar? Likert scale 1 - highest, 5 - lowest. Among the four participants, two answered 4 and the rest answered 1 and 3. Suggesting that our set up was unsatisfactory.
4. Naiintindihan/Nakikita ba ang laman ng bawat slide ng seminar? Likert scale 1 - highest, 5 - lowest. Among the four participants, three answered 1 and the last one answered 2. Suggesting that our slides were satisfactory.
5. Maalam ka na ba sa ligtas na pagkikilos sa internet? Yes / No. Among the four participants, all answered Yes. Suggesting that the first part of our seminar was a success.
6. Maalam ka na ba ng tamang paraan ng pananaliksik ng impormasyon? Yes / No. Among the four participants, all answered Yes. Suggesting that the second part of our seminar was a success.
7. May mga suhestiyon kayong ni nais i-pahayag sa pangkat? I-sulat ang N/A kapag walang nais i-pahayag. Among the four participants, none had any suggestions for the group.
