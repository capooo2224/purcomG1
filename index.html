GROUP 1 SUMMARY

You are visiting the summary page of Group 1's project titled "Empowering Digital Citizens: Building Responsible Digital Practices," which focuses on promoting responsible digital practices among individuals and communities. The project aims to empower digital citizens by providing them with the knowledge, tools, and resources needed to navigate the digital world safely and responsibly. Through a combination of educational initiatives, community engagement, and advocacy efforts, the project seeks to foster a culture of digital responsibility and encourage positive online behavior. By addressing issues such as online privacy, cyberbullying, and digital literacy, the project strives to create a more inclusive and respectful digital environment for all users.
